What is PrintStream?

EFI PrintStream Fulfillment is an intelligent marketing collateral planning and distribution management system. The solution is both powerful and flexible with customization tools and web based applications that allow your customers and your fulfillment team to work together seamlessly and efficiently. Whether you need just basic pick & pack fulfillment features or more complex Print-On-Demand workflows – PrintStream provides a suite of modules to meet your needs.

How does PrintStream Fulfillment work?

Pick & Pack Fulfillment

Manage customer owned or finished goods materials and the pick and pack process from beginning to end for single recipients or distribution lists. PrintStream manages all aspects of order taking and importing, processing, fulfillment, shipping, billing and notifications.

Print-On-Demand Fulfillment

With full support for manufactured and on demand print items, as well as pre-printed inventory, PrintStream makes it simple to integrate the print estimating and job engineering steps to automatically generate production work orders.

Product Fulfillment

PrintStream supports non-print inventory items and materials with easy to manage configuration and inventory level control.

Kitting and Assembly

PrintStream’s comprehensive support for kitting includes everything from light assembly to complex kit builds including variable products and data. PrintStream can track counts, weights and values for individual items as well as resulting kits. PrintStream also track inventory and costs when returned kits require disassembly.

Point of Purchase Displays

Point of Purchase fulfillment offers printers a lucrative value added opportunity by growing customer relationships with a one-stop print and distribute operation. PrintStream supports kits ordered by corporate satellites which can include everything from special event collateral and signage to new store branding and wall displays.

Return Processing

Often overlooked as a revenue stream, the ability to efficiently manage returns of items you’ve shipped (and even items you did not ship) can be an important part of your overall fulfillment strategy. PrintStream is designed to track the disposition of returns, to manage returns with and without an RMA, and to provide customers with a detailed report of return activities and costs.

Automated Fulfillment Events

Automated fulfillment events provide a powerful and flexible method for integrating fulfillment data and transactions with other applications or systems. Fulfillment events can automatically generate user defined emails, reports and file exports that; reduce administrative effort, remove manual touchpoints and reduces errors.

Integration

As a holistic end-to-end integrated solution, PrintStream provides full visibility enabling efficient, profitable management of warehouse and fulfillment operations. With integration to your MIS, ERP or storefront, PrintStream provides the tools to maximize your process and profit potential by incorporating your fulfillment and inventory processes into your manufacturing and accounting processes as one integrated workflow.
